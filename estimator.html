💡 Smart Saving Tips from Stuttgart
🛒

Shop at Aldi & Lidl

Discount supermarkets save you 20–30% vs REWE or EDEKA. Visit Wochenmarkt near closing time for cheap fresh produce.

🏏

Get the Deutschlandticket

€63/month for unlimited public transport across all of Germany. The best transport deal in Europe for students.

🍽

Eat at the Mensa

University canteens serve hot meals for €2–5. Eating there 3–4 times a week can save €60–80/month vs cooking everything from scratch.

👥

Choose WG over Studio

A shared flat saves €200–400/month vs a private studio and also comes with a built-in social circle — great for adjusting to life abroad.

👔

Work as Werkstudent

Up to 20 hrs/week during semester. €12–15/hr is standard. Top sectors: IT, engineering, logistics, tutoring, and campus assistants.

🏭

Apply for BAföG / DAAD

International students may qualify for BAföG support or DAAD scholarships. Apply early — it can cover rent entirely.
